Reimbursements 101

Read on to learn the ins and outs of your organization's tuition reimbursement program.

Good news! Your organization offers tuition reimbursement as an aid to help employees further their career-related education. Each employee may claim up to $1000.00 in tuition reimbursements per calendar year. Reimbursements can cover a percentage of the cost of:

  • University courses (80%)
  • Seminars (60%)
  • Certification preparation classes (75%)
  • Certifications (100%)
  • Technical training (90%)
  • Other educational events (30%)

In order to apply for tuition reimbursement, simply select the 'Create Reimbursement Request' tab on the left and complete the reimbursement request form at least one week prior to your event's start date.

After submission, your request will need to be reviewed and approved by your direct supervisor, then by your department head, and finally, by the Benefits Coordinator. If the request is approved by the Benefits Coordinator, you will be expected to submit a grade or presentation demonstrating knowledge and skills gained from the event. Once this submission has been reviewed and confirmed to satisfy all requirements, your reimbursement will be awarded.
